#2

A famous theorem in math states that only 4 colors are needed to color a map and have not country touch another country of the same color. This proof is famous because it made use of a computer program to go through many examples of maps and see if they could be colored with only 4 colors. This happened in 1977 and many mathematicians didn't accept this proof becuase of its use of a computer.
